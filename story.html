Where Did We Lose Ourselves...? 💔

Once Upon A Time...

We were the kind of couple who couldn't wait to talk. One notification from you could make my entire day. We shared everything... our happiness, our dreams, our future. Back then... love felt effortless.

Then Slowly...

Life became busy. I got lost preparing for my GATE exam. Our conversations became shorter. Replies became slower. Some things were left unsaid. Small misunderstandings slowly became bigger than they ever should have. Neither of us noticed how quietly distance had entered our relationship.

The Truth

I know I wasn't perfect. My fears became bigger than my trust. I kept overthinking. I doubted you more than I should have. And I asked for things that I shouldn't have kept asking for. For all of that... I am truly sorry.

But There Is One Thing I Hope You Know...

This isn't a page to decide who was right or who was wrong. I know there were moments when I felt hurt too. I know there were things we both wish had happened differently. Maybe we both made mistakes. Maybe we both stopped understanding each other. But I don't want those mistakes to become the only thing we remember. Because our story was always bigger than our worst days.

If I Could Change One Thing...

I wouldn't change the day we met. Or our first date. Or our proposal. Or our favourite adventure. I'd only change the moments where we let silence replace communication... where fear replaced trust... and where love got buried under misunderstandings.
